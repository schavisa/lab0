What is Affirmation?

Affirmations are phrases or statements that, when you repeat them regularly (either out loud or to yourself),

can shift negative thought patterns and promote positive thinking, self-esteem, and motivation.

Read More

Ways to practice:

  • Start with 3 to 5 minutes at least twice a day.
  • Repeat each affirmation about 10 times.
  • Ask a trusted loved one to help.
  • Make your routine consistent.
  • Be patient.
