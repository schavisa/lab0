What is Ground?

Cambridge Dictionary:

(n.) the surface of the earth, an area of land used for a particular purpose or activity

(v.) grounded: to be based firmly on something.

From mbgmindfulness:

What does it mean to be grounded?

Being "grounded" means that you're present in your body and connected with the earth.

When you're grounded, you allow yourself to feel centered and balanced no matter what's going on around you.
